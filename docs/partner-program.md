# AgentOS Partner Program

## Overview

The AgentOS Partner Program recognizes developers, agencies, and companies that build on and extend the AgentOS platform. Partners receive benefits based on their tier, including early access to APIs, co-marketing opportunities, and revenue sharing.

---

## Tiers

### Bronze

- **Requirements**: 1 certified plugin published to the Marketplace.
- **Benefits**:
  - Partner badge on Marketplace listings.
  - Access to the Partner Slack channel.
  - Monthly partner newsletter with roadmap previews.

### Silver

- **Requirements**: 3+ certified plugins OR 500+ total installs across plugins.
- **Benefits**:
  - All Bronze benefits.
  - Early access to beta APIs and SDK releases (2 weeks ahead of public).
  - Featured listing rotation on the Marketplace homepage.
  - Dedicated partner support email.

### Gold

- **Requirements**: 5+ certified plugins OR 2,000+ total installs OR 1 enterprise integration.
- **Benefits**:
  - All Silver benefits.
  - Co-marketing opportunities (blog posts, case studies, webinars).
  - 15% revenue share on paid plugin sales.
  - Quarterly roadmap briefing with the AgentOS product team.
  - Priority bug fix SLA (48-hour response).

### Platinum

- **Requirements**: 10+ certified plugins OR 10,000+ total installs OR strategic partnership agreement.
- **Benefits**:
  - All Gold benefits.
  - 25% revenue share on paid plugin sales.
  - Direct Slack channel with AgentOS engineering team.
  - Joint press releases and event sponsorship.
  - Custom API endpoints and dedicated infrastructure support.
  - Annual in-person partner summit invitation.

---

## How to Apply

1. Publish at least one certified plugin to the AgentOS Marketplace.
2. Visit the Partner Portal at `https://partners.agentos.dev` (coming soon).
3. Submit your application with your Marketplace developer profile.
4. Applications are reviewed within 5 business days.

## Tier Advancement

Tier status is evaluated quarterly. Partners are automatically promoted when they meet the next tier's requirements. Tier status is maintained as long as the requirements continue to be met.

## Code of Conduct

All partners must adhere to the AgentOS Developer Code of Conduct, including:
- No malicious or deceptive plugins.
- Respect for user privacy and data protection regulations.
- Transparent pricing for paid plugins.
- Timely response to security vulnerability reports.
